GPT-3 June 2020

The first version of ChatGPT (short for "Chat Generative Pre-trained Transformer") was introduced by OpenAI.It was based on the GPT-3 architecture, which stands for "Generative Pre-trained Transformer 3."

GPT-3 was particularly notable for its size and scale, with 175 billion parameters, making it one of the largest language models available at the time. Its introduction marked a significant milestone in the field of AI and NLP, demonstrating the potential of large-scale transformer models in various applications, including chatbots, content generation, language translation, and more.
